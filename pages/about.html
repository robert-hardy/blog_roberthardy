About

Robert Hardy works as a quant at MAN, a technology-strong investment company based in London, UK.

In his spare time, Robert runs a community of techies/mathematicians that he calls Full Stack Quants. They meet each month at CodeNode, London's premier venue for tech events.

He has been blogging for years about tech, maths and finance. You can find many of his articles on his previous weblog fermatslastspreadsheet.com.
